# Billing Engine - Capabilities

## 1. Domain Purpose
The Billing Engine converts operational events into financial revenue. It is the primary automated bookkeeper for the company's core services.

## 2. Core Capabilities
*   **Tariff Management:** Maintaining complex pricing rules for different lines and vehicles.
*   **Automated Invoice Generation:** Triggered by `TrafficTourCompleted` events.
*   **Bonus Calculation:** Real-time calculation of passenger-based rewards (APC-driven).
*   **Pro-forma Invoicing:** Internal estimates of revenue before final SL settlement.
