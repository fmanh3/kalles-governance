# Payroll Engine - Capabilities

## 1. Domain Purpose
The Payroll Engine calculates and executes salary payments for all staff, ensuring compliance with Swedish labor laws and collective agreements (Bussbranschavtalet).

## 2. Core Capabilities
*   **Automated OB Calculation:** Calculating shift bonuses based on time of day and day of week.
*   **Tax Compliance:** Handling AGI reporting to Skatteverket.
*   **Vacation Accrual:** Tracking earned and used vacation days.
*   **Disbursement:** Generating payment files for the Bank Gateway.
