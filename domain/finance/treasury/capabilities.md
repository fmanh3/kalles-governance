# Treasury & Liquidity - Capabilities

## 1. Domain Purpose
Treasury ensures the company always has enough cash to operate. It provides oversight of liquidity and manages financial risk.

## 2. Core Capabilities
*   **Liquidity Forecasting:** Predicting cash flow for the next 30-90 days.
*   **Cash Position Monitoring:** Real-time visibility into all bank accounts.
*   **Internal Funding:** Managing movements of capital between domain contexts.
