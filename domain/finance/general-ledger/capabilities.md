# General Ledger - Capabilities

## 1. Domain Purpose
The General Ledger is the "Single Source of Truth" for all financial transactions. It maintains the chart of accounts and records every movement of value within the company.

## 2. Core Capabilities
*   **Double-entry Bookkeeping:** Ensuring every debit has a corresponding credit.
*   **Real-time Trial Balance:** Providing an instant view of the company's financial state.
*   **Audit Logging:** Immutable history of all manual and automated entries.
*   **Financial Reporting:** Support for Balance Sheets and P&L statements.
