# Accounts Receivable - Capabilities

## 1. Domain Purpose
Accounts Receivable (Kundreskontra) manages the lifecycle of money owed to the company by its customers (primarily SL).

## 2. Core Capabilities
*   **Payment Tracking:** Monitoring the status of every issued invoice.
*   **Settlement Matching:** Reconciling incoming bank transfers with outstanding receivables.
*   **Aging Analysis:** Reporting on overdue payments and financial risk.
*   **Customer Portal Support:** Providing data for customer-facing dashboards.
